Population and Marriage and Divorce in China

Chinese Population

China is one of the most heavily populated countries in East Asia and the world. It is populated with over one billion people. The graph below breaks down the demographic by male and female from the year 2000 to 2012. The trend shows that there are more men than women living in the country. It also shows that the population for both groups have been steadily rising.
